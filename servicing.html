Annual Interim Service

Our interim car service typically takes about an hour and includes a thorough vehicle check to ensure everything is in optimal condition. As part of the service, we will:

  • Replace the engine oil, oil filter, and sump washer
  • Test the battery
  • Check brake and clutch fluid levels, testing for signs of water absorption
  • Top up screen wash if needed
  • Perform a full vehicle inspection and advise you on any necessary repairs or part replacements

By servicing your car at the correct intervals, you can avoid unexpected issues and keep it running smoothly.

Full Car Service

Our full car service includes everything from the interim service, plus additional maintenance to keep your vehicle in peak condition. This includes:

  • New engine oil, oil filter, and sump washer
  • Top-ups of screen wash, coolant, brake fluid, and clutch fluid
  • Replacement of the fuel filter and spark plugs
  • A comprehensive vehicle check, including battery testing, chassis inspection, and brake servicing
  • An On-Board Diagnostics (OBD) check to identify any hidden issues or error codes

With our expert servicing, you can drive with confidence, knowing your car is in top condition.
